About

Build a tech startup that solves important problems

We provide research-backed advice to early-stage tech entrepreneurs on how to find a startup idea that does as much good as possible.

Book an advice session

What you'll get:

  • Personalised research on the potential social returns of different project ideas, such as our overview of digital financial services for the poor.
  • Strategic advice, including problem selection heuristics, and project design and evaluation. Take a look at some high-level heuristics here.
  • Connection with experts in socially important domains, so you can benefit from what's already known about the problem.
  • A friendly sounding-board for ideas.
